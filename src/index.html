Capabilities

Consulting

Harnessing our combined experience of 150 years, Matogen AI is ideally suited to assist organisations in solving business problems and achieving results.

Data Science

Matogen AI has a team of highly skilled data scientists who leverage international best practices to create powerful predictive models that increase profitability and competitiveness. Our team has produced a range of cutting-edge solutions within the finance, agriculture and health sectors.

Data Analytics

Data is explored and visualised efficiently and effectively to uncover trends and impactful insights. Our team is also actively involved in clients’ business intelligence operations to facilitate executive decision-making.

Data Architecture & Data Engineering

For companies who have their own modelling teams, MAI is adept in combining and enriching raw data in innovative ways to create new features rendering for advanced analytics. In addition, MAI can provide expert guidelines on data pipelines, storage and processing.

Systems Solutions

Capitalising on our foundation as a software development company, Matogen AI is in a strong position to offer an interdisciplinary approach to conceptualise and construct data-driven business solutions, ranging from highly customisable dashboards as powerful information management tools to bespoke data analytics pipelines.

Artificial Intelligence

Matogen Applied Insights has spearheaded groundbreaking AI projects ranging from computer vision in the agriculture industry, neural nets, support vector machines and extreme boosted trees for finance, to fitting a compartmental model for COVID data.
